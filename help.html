How to Play

1. Player 1 will enter two numbers in the text inputs.

2. Player 2 will then multiply the two numbers and put his/her answer in a text input that appears.

3. Player 2 will be given a point if the answer is correct.

4. Then Player 2 will enter the numbers and Player 1 will have to multiply them.

5. This will continue until the players decide to stop playing.
